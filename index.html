Famous Historical Forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Raigad Fort

Located in Maharashtra, it was the capital fort of Shivaji Maharaj and the site of his coronation in 1674.

Sinhagad Fort

Famous for the heroic battle fought by Tanaji Malusare, a general under Shivaji Maharaj, in 1670.

Shivneri Fort

Located in Pune, it is the birthplace of Shivaji Maharaj, built by the Nizam Shahi rulers of Ahmednagar.

Pratapgad Fort

Famous for the battle fought between Shivaji Maharaj and Bijapur's forces in 1659, where Shivaji emerged victorious.

Rajgad Fort

Located near Pune, Rajgad was Shivaji Maharaj's first capital before Raigad Fort.

Torna Fort

One of the first forts captured by Shivaji Maharaj in 1645, it holds strategic importance in Maratha history.

Sindhudurg Fort

Sindhudurg Fort served as a crucial naval base for the Maratha kingdom, protecting the coastline from foreign invasions and pirates.

Vijaydurg Fort

A coastal fort, it was heavily fortified during the reign of Shivaji Maharaj, offering protection against Portuguese and English invasions.
